View/Hide the history of status changes: drag the history panel up/down.

For every status change, the history panel displays:

  1. Timestamp.
  2. Parameter name.
  3. Old status > New status.

Navigate through change list: swipe up/down.

Every status change may also display additional info.

Display additional info for status change: Tap the status change entry.

Hide additional info: Tap the header of status change entry.
