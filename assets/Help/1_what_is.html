MoSKito-Control App shows the status of your application, reporting the current health state of its components.

Being the mobile part of MoSKito-Control, the App is simple yet very effective. This is how everything is set up:

  1. Your application does its work,
  2. MoSKito-Essential collects performance data from your application,
  3. MoSKito-Control WebApp (Server) fetches the needed data from MoSKito-Essential,
  4. MoSKito-Control App receives data from MoSKito-Control WebApp and gracefully displays it on your iOS device.

As a result, you see the major performance indicators of your application at any moment, without accessing your workstation.
