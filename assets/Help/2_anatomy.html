MoSKito-Control App has 4 panels:

  1. Main panel displays monitored components and their health indicators.
  2. Settings panel is used for specifying:
    • MoSKito-Control WebApp address and port,
    • login credentials,
    • intervals for updating data.
  3. Applications panel lists all monitored applications.
  4. History panel shows how the status of monitored applications changed with time.
  5. Top panel contains the essential control elements.
